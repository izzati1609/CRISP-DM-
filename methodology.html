The CRISP-DM Methodology

Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining

Definition

A structured approach to planning and executing a data science project. It serves as the standard blueprint for data mining success.

Origin

Conceived in 1996 by DaimlerChrysler, SPSS, and NCR. The goal was to create an industry-neutral standard process.

The Lifecycle Diagram

The 6 Phases of the Lifecycle

Click on any card below to view detailed Activities & Outputs

1. Business Understanding

Focuses on understanding the project objectives and requirements from a business perspective.

View Activities & Output
  • Activities: Determine objectives, assess situation, produce project plan.
  • Output: Preliminary Project Plan.
2. Data Understanding

Starts with initial data collection and activities to get familiar with the data.

View Activities & Output
  • Activities: Collect data, describe data, explore data (EDA).
  • Output: Data Quality Report.
3. Data Preparation

Constructs the final dataset from the initial raw data (Cleaning & Formatting).

View Activities & Output
  • Activities: Select, clean, construct, and integrate data.
  • Output: Final Dataset.
4. Modeling

Various modeling techniques are selected and applied, and parameters are calibrated.

View Activities & Output
  • Activities: Select technique, build model, assess model.
  • Output: Predictive Model.
5. Evaluation

Evaluate the model specifically against the business objectives.

View Activities & Output
  • Activities: Evaluate results, review process, determine next steps.
  • Output: Final Report.
6. Deployment

Knowledge gained is organized and presented for customer use.

View Activities & Output
  • Activities: Plan deployment, monitoring & maintenance.
  • Output: Live Dashboard / Final Product.
Advantages
  • Flexibility: Industry-neutral application.
  • Iterative: Allows teams to revisit steps.
  • Business Focused: Solves real problems.
Limitations
  • Time-Consuming: Heavy documentation.
  • Rigid: Can be slow for startups.
  • Outdated: Lacks modern MLOps details.
